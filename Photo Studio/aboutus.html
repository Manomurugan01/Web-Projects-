COMPANY PROFILE

intenim Technologies Pvt Ltd, was founded in Nov17 as an IT Products and Services Company to focus entirely on building AI based solutions. Few months later i.e. in Jan’18 company also ventured into eCommerce domain and launched brand VOILA™ which is eCommerce business consultancy services vertical of Intenim Technologies Pvt Ltd. Today, VOILA™ has many verticals, namely; VOILA BIZ, VOILA STUDIO, VOILA RETAIL, VOILA KART and VOILAUX.

Intenim Technologies was awarded the prestigious tag of “Emerging Company of 2020” by Indian Achievers Form in Nov20. On eCommerce business side, VOILA™ is an Authorized Channel Partner for Alibaba.com in India since Jun20. Seller Onboarding and Training Partner for Amazon.in since May18, Seller Services and PLA partner for Flipkart.com, Onboarding Partner for Ajio.com etc. VOILA™ BIZ: eCommerce Consultancy Services Vertical which provides one stop solution for online Retail and wholesale. Services include, Seller Registration, Listing and Cataloguing, Product and Model Photography Services, Account Management Services, Warehousing Services, e-Distributorship, Website Designing, Digital and Social Media Marketing, Banner making and Brand Management Services. VOILABIZ was recognized as amongst Top 10 eCommerce Consultancy Services Company in India by SiliconIndia Magazine for Year 2019. VOILA™ STUDIO: eCommerce Photoshoot Services for all types of Products and Apparels. Studio Approved and Authorized by multiple eCommerce platforms in India e.g. Myntra, Amazon, Flipkart etc VOILA™ KART: eCommerce Multi-Vendor platform to sell products, exclusively for sellers who are working with VOILA and taking VOILA services.

VOILAUX™: Omni Channel concept for Brands. VOILA™ provides physical stores at premium locations in India at zero rental and zero maintenance cost to budding designers and brands selling Men’s and Women’s Apparels and Accessories in India already or desirous to sell and expand business in India. VOILA™ RETAIL: VOILA™ is seller aggregator on many eCommerce platforms and selling products in categories like Shoes, Apparels, Fashion Accessories, Beauty, Auto Accessories, Luggage, Health and Personal Care etc.
